This Privacy Policy describes how MDWebView ("the Application," "we," "us," or "our") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data Collection and Usage by the MDWebView Application

The MDWebView application itself is designed as a minimalist web browser and does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information from your device. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or browse history.

Any settings you configure within the application (such as your custom home page URL, zoom preferences, or cookie settings) are stored locally on your device using `SharedPreferences` and are not transmitted to us or any third parties.

2. Data Collection and Usage by Websites Loaded within the WebView

MDWebView functions as a browser to display content from websites you choose to visit. Please be aware that the websites you access through MDWebView may collect, store, and process your data independently.

  • Information Collected by Websites: When you visit a website via MDWebView, that website may collect various types of information, including:
    • Personal Data: If you submit forms, create accounts, or interact with services on those websites (e.g., your name, email, payment information).
    • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with the website, such as pages visited, links clicked, time spent on pages, and your IP address.
    • Device Information: Details about your device (e.g., browser type, operating system) that websites automatically log.
  • How Websites Use Information: The use of information collected by third-party websites is governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every website you visit, especially before providing any personal information. MDWebView has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.
  • Protection of Information by Websites: The security of your information on third-party websites depends entirely on those websites' practices.
  • Sharing with Third Parties by Websites: Websites you visit may share your data with their own third-party partners (e.g., advertisers, analytics providers) according to their privacy policies.

3. Use of Cookies

  • By the MDWebView Application: The MDWebView application itself does not use cookies for tracking or personal data collection. It provides settings (via `CookieManager`) that allow you to control how cookies are handled by the WebView component for the websites you visit.
  • By Websites Loaded in WebView: Websites you visit will use cookies (both first-party and third-party) for various purposes, such as:
    • Maintaining your login session.
    • Remembering your preferences.
    • Tracking your browse behavior for analytics or advertising.
    • Enabling certain website functionalities.

    Your settings within MDWebView allow you to influence the acceptance of these cookies (e.g., "Allow all cookies," "Allow third-party cookies").

4. Security

While the MDWebView application itself does not collect your data, we are committed to ensuring the security of the application code itself. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of information on websites you visit.

5. Links to Other Sites

MDWebView is designed to access external websites. If you click on a link to another site, you will be directed to that site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

6. User Controls and Your Choices

MDWebView provides you with settings within the application to control certain aspects of your browse experience, including:

  • Setting your preferred home page.
  • Enabling/disabling zoom.
  • Controlling screen rotation.
  • Managing cookie acceptance (all cookies, third-party cookies).
  • Enabling dark mode.
  • Password protecting your settings.

These controls are handled locally on your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the application or through other appropriate means. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
